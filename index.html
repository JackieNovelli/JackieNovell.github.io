Quick About Cepheid Variable

Cepheid Variable stands as a Texas A&M Student Organization devoted to the support and promotion of all things science fiction, fantasy, horror, science, and technology, and the support of the community that grows around them through an atmosphere of acceptance and kinship. Cepheid Variable seeks to promote creativity, enrich imagination, and build active learning opportunities through a variety of educational programs, demonstrations, and presentations.

Small Programs

Cepheid has many small programs to cater to your every nerd. Each small program has their own events or meeting times on top of the Cepheid Meetings. Contact a officer for the events you want! The following is a list of current and active small programs.

Name Description
Attack Of Opportunity DnD and tabletop gaming
For Shirts and Goggles Cosplay, Art, Video, and Photo

Fight Night Girls Small Group

Meetings!

How to Join

Cepheid Variable is a great college club at Texas A&M University. It is for all students and alumni, or as we call them, elders. Cepheid does not have many requirements to join its members in the adventures of nerds n college. The following are the steps to join.

  • Show up to meetings
  • Pay the dues
  • Participate in events
  • Help with fundraising
  • Have fun!
