Events

Cepheid Variable hosts many small and large events for student and non-students. We host two main non-profit events per year and give all the proceeds to Scotty's House, our donation organization. If you would like to know more, please visit our About Page! Otherwise here is some of our main events explained. To get involved, please email us for more information.

Fall MSC Open House

The Fall and Spring MSC Open House is one of our main tabling events to get students and alumni involved in the organization. The following Tuesday is usually our Fish, or Freshman meeting. At that time, we explain who we are, what we do, and what kind of things we do for our members.

Convention Trips

During each semester and sometimes into the summer, we take our members to experience popular convention across Texas. This way our fellow nerds can learn more about conventions, hotel booking, and public cosplay. Many of our members want to get involved in convention planning, so this and our annual con, AggieCon, helps our members have an interactive experience.

Board Game Days

Board game days are days that our members can play any table top game they can imagine. Cepheid has over 300+ board games and plenty of DM's for Dungeons & Dragon campaigns.

Other Events

In order to give the most to its members, Cepheid tries to go to as many places and events as possible. We go to the State Fair of Texas, Conventions, downtown Houston, restarants, and so many more. Check our our social media to see what we are up to!
